Setting up my RtD ToolBox

Before diving into building my future toolbox, I wanted to position myself in the multitude of designerly roles one I can see myself being a part of.

Oscar presented to us Ways of Drifting: Methods of Experimentation in RtD process. The following were the ones that resonated and can possibly serve as explanatory tools for the research questions I want to address. Thnking about this, demanded me to reflect back on my thought process and analyze it carefully.

  • Accumulative (going inwards, depth, from broad to narrow like dissecting a hyperobject theme like climate emergency, tech utopia)
  • Probing (personal pursuit, artistic-based design research, draw connections from different disciplines)

While doing this activity, I realised that I use the accumulative to narrow down to my research brief (contracting) and probing to initiate the relating process by drawing parallels to different disciplines.

Based on the context and the trigger cards I've chosen, the role of protoyping would be more of research archetypes which are more like physical embodiments of concepts as a ways of contributing to the discipline. Something that is more symbolic in nature and also present a 'catalogue of possiblities'

My Design Space:
objects/initiatives that represent the issue I'm enquiring in a tangible way (projects, pictures, keyword, blueprints)
My Design Experiment
Goal: How to highlight the invisible connections between actions and consequences while using digital communication technologies.

In order to collapse the illusion of 'now' and 'then' that is created while using the devices; I borrowed principles from anti-othering. It makes one think about the relationship of casualities holistically (like an entangelment of web you are part of). This series re-imagines ways of making 'time' and 'space' redundant.
